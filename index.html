studio ethos

My practice explores the fragile dialogue between organic forms and digital frontiers. Each piece is a meditation on memory, imperfection, and the poetry of erosion. Based between Berlin and Reykjavik, I work with charcoal, acrylics, and algorithmic brushes — always seeking the uncanny intersection of hand and code.

Selected exhibitions: *Matter of Time* (Reykjavik Art Museum), *Phantom Terrains* (Online/ Berlin Biennale). Commissions and collaborations welcome.
